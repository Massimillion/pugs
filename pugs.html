About Pugs

Pugs are an ancient breed that originated in China over 2,000 years ago. These charming companions were once the pets of Chinese emperors and later became favorites of European royalty.

Known for their distinctive wrinkled faces, curly tails, and expressive eyes, pugs are beloved for their playful personalities and unwavering loyalty. Despite their small size, they have big hearts and even bigger personalities.
